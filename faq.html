Placing an order is simple: browse products on the main shop page, select the items you wish to purchase, and add them to your cart. Once finished, open the cart, verify your items, input your shipping address details, and complete the payment using UPI.
You can monitor your order status in real time. Navigate to the **Profile** page and click **My Orders**. You will see a live 4-step progress tracker: **Placed** ➔ **Approved/Rejected** ➔ **Out for Delivery** ➔ **Delivered**.

We primarily support secure digital UPI payments. At checkout, after validating your shipping address, a personalized dynamic UPI payment QR code is generated. You can scan it using any major UPI app (BHIM, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) to complete your transaction instantly.
The Cenin Wallet stores your promotional credits and order refund cashbacks. Any time you cancel an eligible order, the refund value is credited back instantly to your Cenin Wallet balance. You can apply this balance at checkout to get discounts or pay for orders completely.
No, wallet balances are store credits and are non-transferable. They cannot be withdrawn to bank accounts, card accounts, or third-party UPI addresses. They are only applicable for purchases on Cenin.
Active orders can be cancelled prior to dispatch. Open your **Profile** page, select the **Cancel Order** option from the menu, pick the order ID from your list, choose a cancellation reason, and click **Confirm Cancellation**. Double cancellations on the same order are strictly prevented.
Cenin supports instant refunds. Once your cancellation request is verified, 100% of the purchase amount is credited directly back to your Cenin Wallet immediately.
Google authentication ensures secure access to your personal details. It guards your active orders history, protects your Cenin Wallet balance against unauthorized access, and secures your saved shipping addresses.
Under your **Profile** page, navigate to **Saved Addresses**. Here, you will see your default address card and any additional addresses. You can click **Edit** or **Delete** on any card. A custom confirmation modal dialog will prompt you to confirm changes to prevent accidental deletions.
