Privacy & Terms

Effective Date: January 2024

1. Nature of the Platform

Welcome to Cenin. Please be advised that Cenin operates strictly as a marketplace platform. We do not manufacture, design, or directly own the products listed on our website. We collaborate with multiple third-party partners and suppliers to source and deliver products to you.

Our primary mission is to decrease the retail markup and provide our customers with high-quality goods at the most accessible prices possible. Because we rely on external suppliers, the exact material, build, and composition of any product are completely dependent on the respective supplier.

Important Material Disclaimer: Cenin does not sell genuine gold, authentic silver, or real diamond-coated products. Any product descriptions implying such are for aesthetic, stylistic, and color reference only.

2. Order Cancellations

We strive to process orders immediately to ensure fast delivery. Therefore, we generally do not accept order cancellations.

However, if you wish to cancel your order, you must submit a cancellation request within exactly 24 hours of order placement. If approved, the refunded amount will be credited exclusively to your Cenin Wallet. We do not process cancellation refunds to original bank accounts, credit cards, or UPI methods under any circumstances.

3. Return & Refund Policy

Due to the highly discounted nature of our platform, we do not accept returns. All sales are considered final. We will only review requests for exceptions in the event of severe transit damage or receiving an entirely incorrect product.

  • Unboxing Video Required: To be eligible for a review, you must provide a clear, uncut, and unedited unboxing video of the package showing the shipping label and the defect.
  • Resolution Process: If your proof is validated and the reason is deemed correct by our internal team, we may issue a 60% refund of the product value to your Cenin Wallet.
  • In such approved cases, we will issue the 60% refund without requiring you to ship the physical product back to us.

4. Influencer Discounts & Promotions

If you purchase an item using a referral link, promo code, or discount provided by a social media influencer, please note that the discounted amount holds no cash value. In the rare event of a refund or partial refund, the influencer's discount amount is strictly excluded from the calculation.

5. Marketing & Agency Partners

All official marketing, promotional campaigns, creator crew programs, and brand partnerships for Cenin are actively managed and overseen by our designated marketing agency, EliteX. EliteX is authorized to handle public relations and influencer onboarding on our behalf.

6. Privacy, Cookies & Data Protection

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. We adhere to strict data protection standards:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information (including name, phone number, and address) with any unauthorized third-party partners or external entities.
  • Authentication: We use Google Sign-In and email verification protocols strictly to verify your identity, secure your Cenin Wallet, and process your orders safely.
  • Cookies: Our platform utilizes cookies to maintain your session, keep items in your cart, and provide a seamless browsing experience. By using Cenin, you consent to our use of essential cookies.
